Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that you may have some questions about our products and services at Woman and Child, so we've compiled a list of the most commonly asked questions and answers for your convenience.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept all major credit cards, as well as PayPal.

Q: How long will it take for my order to arrive?

A: We aim to process and ship all orders within 1-2 business days, and shipping times vary depending on your location. Please refer to our shipping policy for more information.

Q: Do you offer international shipping?

A: Yes, we do offer international shipping. Please note that additional fees and taxes may apply, depending on your country's regulations.

Q: What is your return policy?

A: We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase from Woman and Child. If for any reason you're not happy with your order, please contact us within 14 days of receiving it to arrange a return or exchange. Please refer to our return policy for more information.

Q: How can I get in touch with your customer service team?

A: You can contact us via email or phone, and we'll do our best to respond to your inquiry within 24 hours. Our contact information can be found on the "Contact Us" page of our website.

We hope this FAQ section has been helpful. If you have any other questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.
